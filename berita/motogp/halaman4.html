Dovizioso: the attendance king

The italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage fot 16 straight years - the third on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-year-old, who in the las two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) mosst consistent challenger.
